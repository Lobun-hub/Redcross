Who We Are

A Community Built on Humanity & Service

The Kirinyaga University Red Cross Chapter is a student-led humanitarian organization committed to promoting the seven fundamental principles of the Red Cross Movement.

🩸 First Aid

Training and responding to medical emergencies on and off campus.

💧 Blood Drives

Organizing donation campaigns to support national blood banks.

🤝 Community Service

Reaching out to vulnerable groups in the surrounding community.

🎓 Capacity Building

Empowering students through workshops, drills, and leadership programs.

🌍

Our Mission

To inspire, encourage, and initiate at all times all forms of humanitarian activities by the Kirinyaga University community so as to prevent and alleviate human suffering.
