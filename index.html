Our Foundation

The 7 Fundamental Principles

The Kenya Red Cross Society is guided by these seven principles of the Red Cross Red Crescent Movement.

❤️

Humanity

To prevent and alleviate human suffering wherever it may be found.

⚖️

Impartiality

No discrimination based on nationality, race, religion, class or political opinions.

🕊️

Neutrality

Refraining from taking sides in hostilities or engaging in controversies.

🛡️

Independence

Autonomous and independent from governments and institutions.

🙌

Voluntary Service

A voluntary relief movement not prompted by desire for gain.

🌐

Unity

There can only be one Red Cross or Red Crescent Society in any one country.

🌍

Universality

A worldwide institution where all Societies have equal status.

✊

Our Commitment

At KYU Red Cross we uphold all seven principles in every action we take.
